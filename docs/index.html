We know about three elemental natures.

Each tower and enemy has an elemental nature: FIRE, WATER or EARTH. Fire towers deal extra damage against earth foes, but are weak against water ones.

YOU have four tower types

Basic Tower: All-rounder. Has average fire speed and damage.
Cannon Tower: Devastating. Fires very damaging bullets, but is very slow.
Haste Tower: Nimble. Incredible fire rate, but low damage output.
Area Tower: You shall not pass. Low damage and speed, but hits every enemy in range!

They are three types of enemies.

Minion: horde. They aren't special, but they come in big groups and can overwhelm your targeting towers.
Skeleton: Undead soldier. No matter how hard you hit them, they have shields! You will need many shots instead of powerful ones.
Golem: Healing Relict. Hit them hard, hit them fast. These monsters can heal over time!

Place towers

If you have enough gold, you can place a tower by left-clicking and dragging the icons on screen. To sell a tower, click it with your mouse-wheel and recover half of cost .
